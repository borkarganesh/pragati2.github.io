A protocol stack is a collection (or layered set) of communication protocols that work together to enable data transmission between devices.

Each layer in the stack has a specific role, and they interact in a hierarchical way ‚Äî the output of one layer becomes the input of the next. Think of it like a team where each member has a specific job.

A protocol stack is a group of rules (protocols) organized in layers that help two devices communicate smoothly.
